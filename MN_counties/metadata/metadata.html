Section 1 Overview
Originator Minnesota Department of Transportation
Title County Boundaries in Minnesota
Abstract This dataset represents the county boundaries, as recognized by the Minnesota Department of Transportation. There are 87 counties in Minnesota.
Purpose The purpose of this dataset is to spatially represent the county boundaries and use as reference for other layers spatially dependent on county boundaries (ex. ATP boundaries).

Section 2 Data Quality
Attribute Accuracy
Logical Consistency Topology Rules for: Gaps, Overlaps, Covered By State
Completeness Complete
Horizontal Positional Accuracy All of the data sources reviewed and the final dataset were within the National Map Accuracy Standards for 1:24,000-scale maps which is +/- 40 feet (12 meters). The dataset is not intended for legal land survey use, and is best suited for general reference.
Vertical Positional Accuracy Not Applicable
Lineage This dataset was originally developed by the Minnesota Department of Natural Resources, then adjusted by MnDOT staff to match county boundaries more accurately using high resolution imagery, USGS boundary interpretations, and interpretations from local sources.
